about vim

Vim is the name for the graphic design of Martin Coote. Vim works in brand, print and web applications, answering the needs of clients and their audiences with elegant solutions. Martin has been working in the creative industries for nearly a decade.

The lagomorph, Oryctolagus cuniculus, is named Binky.

brand identity

Life MOT

The CIC LifeMOT uses therapy techniques to allow people to look after themselves in the same way they would look after their car. The marque references the MOT symbol using colour to reflect the humanity and variety of the service.

Diamonds are Forever

A Marie Curie Cancer Care charity ball. Diamonds are Forever brings the sophistication of Classic James Bond films to the Atlantic Hotel in Newquay. This marque is a visual pun on the event name.

with care

design production

Scratch Magazine

Client: Nixon Design

Scratch is one of a number of print projects undertaken for Nixon Design. A large format alumni magazine with paper sponsorship from Fedrigoni, featuring a 5th colour metallic gold throughout and three paper stocks (uncoated, gold and coated).

Keltek Brewery Bottle Labels

Client: Gendall Design

Designed by Gendall and featuring illustrations by Ceri Pashley, these labels combine a contemporary brewery feel with a comic book edge. Silver foil highlights the logo and elements within the illustrations, giving a kick to the medieval weapons that mirrors the ales themselves.
